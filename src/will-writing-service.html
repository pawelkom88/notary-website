WILL WRITING SERVICE


When it comes to planning for the future and safeguarding your legacy, a professionally drafted and notarised Will is an essential document. In the United Kingdom, a notarised Will holds significant value, providing you and your loved ones with peace of mind, legal clarity, and assurance that your wishes will be respected.


Creating a Will is a thoughtful and responsible act that allows you to determine how your assets are distributed and your affairs managed after you're gone. Sikorska Notary offers a structured and reliable Will writing process:



  • Step 1: Initial Inquiry

  • Contact me to express your interest in creating a Will.

  • Step 2: Completion of Questionnaire

  • You'll receive a questionnaire to fill out, where you are expected to provide honest and clear informa

  • Submit the completed questionnaire to me via email ps@sikorskanotary.co.uk.

  • Step 3: Cost Confirmation

  • Upon receiving your questionnaire, I assess your needs and confirm the cost of the Will writing service.

  • There are two options available:

    • Basic Will (£120)

    • For couples, two identical (mirror) wills are available for a total of £200.
    • Premium Will (£250)

    • For couples, two identical (mirror) wills are available for a total of £400.


  • A 50% deposit of the total cost is required at this stage.

  • Step 4: Comprehensive Case Assessment

  • I conduct a thorough assessment of your case based on the information you've provided.

  • An email is sent to you outlining the available options and recommendations.

  • Step 5: Further Clarifications

  • If you have additional questions or require further explanations, I arrange a meeting to discuss your preferences and requirements in detail.

  • Step 6: Drafting and Review of Wills

  • Once you confirm terms of your Will, I proceed to draft your Will.

  • You receive draft copies of your Will(s) for careful review and approval.

  • Step 7: In-Person Will Signing

  • I schedule an in-person meeting with you and two witnesses for the formal signing of your Will(s), ensuring they are legally binding.

  • Optional Step: Registering the Will

  • If you wish, I can assist in registering your Will with The National Will Register for an additional fee of £30.

  • The National Will Register exists to ensure no Will is left unknown or untraced at the time it is needed.

  • Step 8: Post-Service Support

  • I remain available to address any post-service questions or concerns you may have, ensuring your peace of mind.

Contact me today to take the first step towards securing your legacy and protecting your loved ones' future.
